About

A detail-oriented Front-End Web Developer able to strategize and prioritize effectively to problem solve under pressure, with applicable hands on experience in JavaScript, HTML/CSS, Bootstrap and jQuery through the development of several projects.

I have wide variety of work experience ranging from Automotive Mechanic to a team Leader at a Marketing Firm. I have always been very interested in computer science since high school so I started researching the career in detail and teaching myself how to program using online learning tools. I learned to love programming so much that I started coding for 9+ hours everyday, building all sorts of web pages and functional applications.

Although I currently only program front end web pages and web applications my true goal is to expand my skills enough to work as a Software Engineer at a space exploration company such as SpaceX or NASA Goddard in the future. As a young child I would look up into the night sky and dream of sending a rocket to outer space. I believe programming will be the key to allow me to reach my dreams, this is just the beginning of my journey.
